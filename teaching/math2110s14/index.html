Mastering concepts such as vector fields, partial derivatives, and multiple integrals will allow you to further your study in higher mathematics, physics, biology, chemistry, engineering, and economics.
  • Course expectations:
    • You are expected to attend all class sessions. I will lecture on Tuesdays and Thursdays, while the Wednesday classes are devoted to cooperative problem-solving.
    • It goes without saying that you need to practice often in order to internalize the concepts and master the mechanics of calculus! Therefore, attack the homework assignments as early and diligently as possible! "Strike while the iron is hot."
    • Form study groups! Learn how to articulate your ideas and reasonings to your classmates/friends. "The best way to learn is to teach."
